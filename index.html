Are You In Pain?



Have you ever been having chronic muscle pain and no matter what you do to alleviate it, the pain comes back. Well what if I told you that the source of the pain was in a different area. The pain that you feel can be a referral pain and one way to help address the issue is known as "Trigger Point Therapy." Trigger points are points in the muscle area that are too tight most commonly known by many as "knots." As the muscle tightens, it constrict the bloodflow which results in a lack of oxygen being delivered to the area. This would the result in the muscle pulling other areas of the same muscle as it tightens causing discomfort or pain in both areas.
